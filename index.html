The Reality

Little Rock's best food
is hiding in plain sight.

There are restaurants in Little Rock that have been feeding this community for 10, 15, even 20 years. They have regulars who love them. Food that tells a story. A neighborhood that depends on them.

But if you search for them online — nothing comes up.

No website. No hours. No menu. Most customers search online before they eat. If you're not there, you're invisible — and that's not fair to you or the people who would love your food.

Building a website feels expensive, complicated, or just one more thing on an already full plate. You shouldn't have to choose between running your business and being found online.

"A customer asked for my website and I had nothing to show them."

— The kind of story this program exists to change

The Process

Simple from start
to launch.

01
We meet — you tell me your story
One 30–60 minute kickoff conversation, virtual or in person in Little Rock. You tell me about your business and what you want people to feel when they find you online.
02
I build your site
I take everything we discussed and build your website. Clean, professional, and mobile-friendly. Then I send it to you for review.
03
You review and send edits
You have 10 days to look it over and send back your feedback. No request is too small.
04
We refine it together
Up to 2–3 rounds of refinements until it feels exactly right. This is your site — it should feel like you.
05
We launch — you're open
Your business goes live online. I stay with you for 1–2 hours a month for three months. After six months, the site is fully yours.
