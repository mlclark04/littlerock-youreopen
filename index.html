The Process

Simple from start
to launch.

01
We meet — you tell me your story
One 30–60 minute kickoff conversation, virtual or in person in Little Rock. You tell me about your business and what you want people to feel when they find you online.
02
I build your site
I take everything we discussed and build your website. Clean, professional, and mobile-friendly. Then I send it to you for review.
03
You review and send edits
You have 10 days to look it over and send back your feedback. No request is too small.
04
We refine it together
Up to 2–3 rounds of refinements until it feels exactly right. This is your site — it should feel like you.
05
We launch — you're open
Your business goes live online. I stay with you for 1–2 hours a month for three months. After six months, the site is fully yours.
